🌊 Diphthong Dash

Sort the word into the right sound bucket!

Choose your level
🎮What: A pattern-sorting game — sort each word into the bucket that matches its vowel-team spelling.
👆How to play: A word appears (like COIN or BOY). Tap the correct bucket: OI, OY, OU, or OW. Easy sorts two buckets; Hard uses all four.
🧠Helps with: Diphthong recognition — sliding vowel sounds (oi, ow) are common but tricky because two spellings share one sound. Sorting by spelling pattern builds the visual memory readers need.
